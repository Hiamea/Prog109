Dubai

Dubai is a city in the United Arab Emirates. It is a city that has been constructed not even 50 years ago. It is a city known for its luxury shopping, ultramodern architecture and nightlife scene. The tallest building in the world, Burj Khalifa, which is 830m-tall tower, is also found here .Dubai also has artificial beaches and islands
